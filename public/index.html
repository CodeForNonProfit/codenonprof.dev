Why Nonprofit?

We work with nonprofits because many nonprofit organizations have little funds to spend on high-quality web design services. Some nonprofits don't even have a presecence on the web. By choosing to work with a nonprofit organization, no matter their physical location, we extend the reach of their impact.

Our Approach

We want to bring people together to increase their strength and power. We are united in two goals. 1. Provide nonprofits organizations with high quality web design so they can continue generating measurable changes in their communities. 2. Present web developers, both new and experienced, with opportunities to build upon their skills while contributing to the positive impact nonprofits make in their communities.

How it Works

We want to keep things as simple as possible. Anyone can submit the name of a nonprofit organization. We will ask you a few questions about the nonprofit and within a few days, we can add it to our project list. Developers looking to donate their time and talent, can then view all open projects and request to work on the new web design. We ask the developer a few questions about their skills and experience. We then make sure the project they are interested in is available and then we let them do what they do best! When the site is ready, we contact the nonprofit organization.

Who We Are

Each team member has a differnt story of how they started with CoceForNonProfit. Some of us have been involved with nonprofits and some of us are involved in the web design industry. We come from very unique backgrounds are are united by a desire to build quality websites for inspirational nonprofits and provide developers with the chance to enhance their skills.
